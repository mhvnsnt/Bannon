# God Within — non-canon roster (game-only, parallel to canon)

Onyx and her stable are **game-only** — they do NOT appear in the six books (the only "Onyx" in the
manuscripts is Club Onyx, a Miami nightclub in Book 6). That's by design: this mode is 100% game-only, no
canon to retrofit. Theme: characters who, like Maime, are **not running on the numerology engine** everyone
else in the universe runs on.

## ONYX — "The Obsidian Hex"  (registered: `_addChar('ONYX', … archetype:'striker')`)
Black woman, white chola/vamp facepaint, spiked leather. ~5 attires (see `../../assets/models/CANON_MODELS.md`:
`ONYX.glb` chola/spiked, `ONYX_corset.glb` corset/vamp; more on the way).
- **Identity:** her Life Path is not *unknown* — it's **uncomputable**. She exists outside whatever assigns a
  number in this universe. Marquis can't perceive her because his read on people is tuned to the numerology OS;
  she isn't hiding — she's a blind spot in how he sees.
- **Agenda:** the system itself (Life-Path-as-destiny, numbers-as-cage) is the problem, and Maime — a self born
  from trauma in a jail cell, never assigned a number — is her proof of concept. She's *midwifing* the first
  person who gets to exist without a number deciding him in advance. Genuinely gray, not mustache-twirl.
- **In-ring:** **Denial Grappler** — doesn't out-power, she erases what you've built (counters + momentum-theft,
  near-zero startup repositioning, can cancel out of hitstun on rare windows; lower raw damage — wins on
  information denial). Finisher **The Vacancy** — no setup, ends the sequence mid-motion.

## The stable (all off the numerology engine)
- **CIPHER** — Anti-Pattern Brawler; Life Path recalculates to a different digit every read. Offense genuinely
  non-deterministic move-to-move (real RNG in attack selection — nothing else in the roster does this).
  Finisher **The Undefined** — randomizes between three finishers, no tell.
- **ECHO** — Mimic/Read; no Life Path of her own, hers is whatever the opponent runs. Finisher **The Reflection**
  — no signature; she hits you with your own move. (Doubles as "corrupted-reflection" boss tech.)
- **HOLLOW** — Life Path resolves to a flat **0** (impossible under the universe's rules — a system error in a
  body). Silent Submission Specialist, never an entrance or mic. Onyx's recruiter — targets roster members whose
  own Life Path already failed them, offering exit from the number. Finisher **The Unheard** — sudden guillotine.
- **STATIC** — two conflicting Life Paths superimposed/interfering; visually **flickers/glitches only near Onyx
  or Maime**. Power/Interference. Finisher **The Corrupted Frame** — a powerbomb where the model glitches on impact.

The flicker/glitch tells (STATIC near Onyx/Maime; Onyx rendering only in Maime-state) are the SAME render hook —
model visibility keyed to persona/proximity, no dialogue needed to explain it.
