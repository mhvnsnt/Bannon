
## Simultaneous Execution: MDickie Parser & Morph Previewer
- **MDickie Move Parser**: Built `UBannonMDickieParser` to parse MDickie Unity asset exports, translating structural animation arrays into native `FMovesetSlot` configurations.
- **Real-Time Morph Previewer**: Built `UBannonMorphPreviewer` for the Creation Hub. Dynamically applies morph targets to the viewport mesh with bounds recalculation to support extreme CAW proportions without culling.
